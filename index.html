How to Play

  • Click on highlighted objects to interact with them
  • Collect items and use them to unlock doors
  • Use the inventory drawer on the left to view collected items
  • Click the back arrow to return to the previous view
  • Click the phone in the starting room for hints if you get stuck
  • Find your way out of the Tribble basement to escape!
